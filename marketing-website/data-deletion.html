🔒 Data Deletion Request

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. We take your privacy seriously and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and Google Play data safety requirements.

Your Rights

📋 What Personal Data We Store

The following personal information is collected when you create a Starship Psychics account:

Data Type How It's Used
Name & familiar name Personalising your oracle experience
Email address Account login, security codes, service notifications
Phone number Two-factor authentication (SMS)
Date & place of birth Generating your birth chart and astrological readings
Chat message history Providing your oracle session; retained for legal compliance
IP address Security monitoring, fraud prevention
Billing information Subscription payment processing (Stripe / Google Play)

All data is stored on AWS (United States) and is encrypted both in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest (AES encryption).

Deletion Policy

🗓️ What Happens After You Request Deletion

We follow a two-phase deletion process to balance your privacy rights with legal compliance obligations:

Day 0 — Deletion request submitted
Your account is immediately deactivated. You can cancel this request and reactivate within 30 days by logging back in.
Day 30 — Personal information permanently deleted
Your name, email address, phone number, date/place of birth, billing address, and IP address are permanently and irreversibly deleted. Your account cannot be recovered after this point.
Year 7 — Chat messages permanently deleted
Chat message history is retained for a maximum of 7 years as required by applicable legal retention obligations (e.g., to respond to litigation or regulatory requests). After 7 years the messages are permanently and irreversibly deleted.
🔐 Legal retention note: Chat messages retained after 30 days are stored in an anonymized, encrypted form with no link to your name, email, or any other personal identifier visible in the system. They can only be accessed by company legal counsel in response to a court order or subpoena.
How to Delete

🗑️ How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account and personal data, use the Starship Psychics app you downloaded from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store:

📱

Delete via the App

Open the Starship Psychics app → Settings → Account → Delete Account. You'll receive an email verification code to confirm the request.

Transparency

ℹ️ What We Cannot Delete

Even after your deletion request, we are required by law to retain certain minimal records:

  • Anonymized transaction records — required by financial regulations (no personal identifiers are retained).
  • Deletion audit log — a record that a deletion was processed, containing only a cryptographic hash (not your actual email or name).
  • Encrypted chat messages — retained for up to 7 years as described above, accessible only under legal compulsion and completely separated from your identity.

These retained records contain no name, email address, phone number, birth date, or any other directly identifying information after the 30-day mark.
