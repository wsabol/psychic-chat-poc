## 18. Definitions

**Personal Information**: Any information that identifies or could identify you individually

**PII**: Personally Identifiable Information (name, email, birth date, location)

**Processing**: Any operation performed on data (collection, storage, use, deletion)

**Data Controller**: The entity that decides why/how data is processed (us)

**Data Processor**: Third party that processes data on our behalf (service providers)

**Data Subject**: The individual whose data is being processed (you)

**Consent**: Your voluntary, informed agreement to data processing

**Legitimate Interest**: A reason to process data that benefits you or us
